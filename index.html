Family Meeting Rules

  1. Listen Respectfully: No interruptions. Everyone gets a chance to be fully heard.
  2. Be Open-Minded: It's okay to disagree, but we listen to understand, not just to argue.
  3. Everyone Participates: We all have a voice and are encouraged to share our thoughts and feelings.
  4. Stay Positive & Constructive: We focus on finding solutions and supporting each other, not on blaming.
  5. What's Said Here, Stays Here: This is a safe space. We respect each other's privacy.
